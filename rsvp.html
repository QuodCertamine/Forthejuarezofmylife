DIRECTIONS

Coming from Dallas

Go northbound on I-35E. Turn right onto Loop 288N. Then take the FM 428 exit toward Aubrey. Turn right on to FM 428/E. Sherman Dr. Follow the 6.5 miles and turn right on to Wildcat Rd. Finally follow Wildcat Rd. for 1.7 miles and THE SPRINGS will be on the left.

Coming from Oaklahoma City

Go South on I-35. Take exit 478 and turn left onto FM 455. Follow for 6.3 miles and then turn right onto Running Bear Rd. Turn left onto FM 2153. Follow FM 2153 for 4 miles and then turn left onto FM 428E. Follow for 2 miles and turn right onto Wildcat Rd. Follow for 1.7 miles and The Springs will be on the left.
